About the ECE Department

Welcome to the Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department at Academy Of Technology. Our 4-year course is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of electrical and electronics science and technology. ECE department at AOT is renowned for its academic excellence and process.
