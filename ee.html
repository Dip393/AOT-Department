About the EE Department

Welcome to the Electrical Engineering (EE) Department at Academy Of Technology. Our 4-year course is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of electrical engineering and technology. EE department at AOT is renowned for its academic excellence and process.
