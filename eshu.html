About the Engineering Science & Humanities Department

Welcome to the Engineering Science & Humanities Department at Academy Of Technology. Our 4-year course is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of science and humanities. Engineering Science & Humanities department at AOT is renowned for its academic excellence and process.
